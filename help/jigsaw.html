Speed Duck Games Libre Jigsaw

Jigsaw Help

  1. To pick up a piece (or multiple connected pieces):
    • Move the mouse cursor over the piece you want to pick up.
    • Press and hold the mouse button while the mouse cursor is over the piece.
    • While holding the mouse button, you can move the piece.
    • Drop the piece by releasing the mouse button.
  2. To rotate a piece (or multiple connected pieces):
    • While a piece is picked up (see 1), press the right arrow '→' to rotate clockwise, or the left arrow '←' to rotate counter-clockwise.
      OR (with the mouse):
    • Left-click to rotate counterclockwise. Right-click to rotate clockwise.
  3. To select multiple pieces:
    (Selected pieces cannot be rotated)
    • Move the mouse cursor to an area where there are no pieces.
    • Press the mouse button and "drag" the selection box over the pieces you want.
    • Release the mouse button.
    • Now the selected pieces can be moved together.
      OR
    • Hold down the shift key while clicking on a piece.
    • Pieces can be added to the selection by holding down the shift key.
  4. Layers (more space):
    • The game has three layers to give space for organizing pieces.
    • Switch between these layers using the '1', '2', and '3' keys. Any selected or picked up pieces will be moved to the specified layer.
